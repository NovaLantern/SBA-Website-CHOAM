Rental Agreement

Welcome to CHOAM LLC, where we strive to provide you with high-quality event equipment and exceptional service. Before booking your event rentals, please take a moment to review our rental agreement terms to ensure a smooth and successful rental experience.

1. Rental Duration and Delivery

  • Rental Duration: The standard rental period is 1-5 days, starting from the time of delivery or pickup. Any extension of the rental period must be agreed upon in advance and may incur additional fees.
  • Delivery & Pickup: We offer delivery and pickup services for all rental equipment. Delivery charges vary depending on the location and size of your order. A scheduled delivery window will be agreed upon at the time of booking.

2. Reservation and Payment

  • Booking: To reserve your rental items, a deposit of 20% is required at the time of booking. The remaining balance is due 10 days after the event.
  • Payment Methods: We accept payments via credit card and bank transfer. Payments are non-refundable after the final balance is paid.
  • Cancellations & Refunds: Cancellations made 7 days or more before the scheduled event will receive a 80% refund. No refunds will be given for cancellations made less than 3 days before the event date.

3. Equipment Care and Responsibility

  • Customer Responsibility: You are responsible for the proper care and handling of all rental equipment during the rental period. Any damage, loss, or theft of items while in your possession will be charged to you.
  • Return Condition: All rental items must be returned in the same condition as they were delivered or picked up. Please clean any used equipment and return it promptly after the event. A cleaning fee may be charged for excessively dirty items.
  • Equipment Setup and Use: If setup or breakdown services are required, additional charges will apply. Please ensure the equipment is used for its intended purpose and in accordance with any specific guidelines provided.

4. Damages and Loss

  • Damage Waiver: We offer an optional damage waiver for an additional fee. This waiver covers accidental damage to rental items during your event, excluding damage caused by misuse, negligence, or failure to follow instructions.
  • Repair and Replacement Fees: If rental items are damaged or lost, the customer will be charged the full cost of repair or replacement, including any associated labor and transportation fees.

5. Liability and Insurance

  • Liability: You agree to hold CHOAM LLC harmless from any liability, injury, or loss that may occur during the rental period. It is your responsibility to ensure that the equipment is used safely and in compliance with all local laws and safety regulations.
  • Insurance: We recommend that you obtain event insurance to cover any unforeseen incidents or damages that may occur during your rental period.

6. Indemnification

You agree to indemnify and hold CHOAM LLC harmless from any claims, demands, or legal actions arising from the use or misuse of rented equipment during the rental period.

7. Additional Terms

  • Substitution: If any rented equipment becomes unavailable or unsuitable for your event, we reserve the right to substitute items of equal or greater value without prior notice.
  • Force Majeure: We are not liable for any failure to perform due to circumstances beyond our control, including but not limited to weather events, natural disasters, or government regulations.
